History of TechCon

TechCon began as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in 2005 and has since grown into a global phenomenon, attracting thousands of attendees annually. Over the years, the conference has served as a platform for launching revolutionary technologies and fostering innovation in the industry.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is driven by the mission to inspire innovation, facilitate knowledge sharing, and build a collaborative tech community. By bringing together visionaries and professionals, we aim to shape the future of technology for the betterment of society.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Lovelace, a pioneer in computational theory, has inspired generations with her groundbreaking work. She was a keynote speaker in 2018, discussing the evolution of algorithms.

Mr. Alan Turing

Mr. Turing, a leading figure in artificial intelligence, shared his vision for the future of AI during his 2019 address.

Ms. Grace Hopper

Ms. Hopper captivated audiences in 2020 with her insights on the history and future of programming languages.
